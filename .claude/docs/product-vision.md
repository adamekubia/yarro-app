# Product Vision & ICP

**What Yarro is:**
Yarro is a WhatsApp-native maintenance, compliance, and management platform built specifically for HMO (Houses in Multiple Occupation) operators in the UK. It's the first AI-native platform to combine room-level HMO management, structured WhatsApp dispatch, and compliance automation in a single product.

**The core positioning:**
"Yarro gives HMO operators complete control over their property maintenance by automating coordination so they never have to chase a job or a contractor again."

**Core USP:**
A complete audit trail that protects the operator when things go wrong — council inspections, rent withholding disputes, landlord complaints. Everything logged, timestamped, evidenced. AI-native from day one, not bolted on.

**Target customer (ICP):**
- Landlords and property managers with 5–50 units
- R2R operators, BRRRR landlords, and corporate multi-let operators
- HMO-heavy portfolios (5+ bed shared houses, student lets, professional HMOs)
- Decision maker is the operator themselves. Sales cycle is days not months.
- Pain points: context switching between tools, always chasing contractors, things falling through cracks, no audit trail when disputes arise, landlords losing patience, unreliable contractors, out-of-hours emergencies, approval bottlenecks

**Not targeting (for now):** Letting agencies, general BTL landlords, large corporate operators.

**What makes HMO different from standard lettings:**
- Multiple tenants per property, each in their own room
- Shared areas (kitchen, bathroom, hallway) create ambiguity on who reports what
- Higher compliance burden: HMO licence, fire risk, EICR, gas safety all mandatory
- Room-level tracking matters — which room reported the issue, which tenant, which contractor visited
- Councils inspect HMOs. The audit trail is not a nice-to-have, it's legal protection

**Competitive landscape:**
- **COHO** — room-level accounting, no dispatch. No WhatsApp coordination.
- **Arthur Online** — full workflow, £70+/mo, no WhatsApp. Overkill for small operators.
- **10ninety** — property management software for smaller operators. No AI-native dispatch or WhatsApp coordination.
- **Lettie** — triage + compliance reminders, no dispatch chain. £20/mo for 8 props.
- **DIY WhatsApp groups** — confirms demand, but no structure, no audit trail, nothing logged.

Yarro sits in the gap: AI-native, WhatsApp-first, room-level HMO management with structured dispatch and compliance automation. No competitor combines all three.

**Build decisions should always ask:**
- Does this serve an HMO operator specifically or is it generic?
- Does this reduce chasing, context switching, or compliance risk?
- Does this add to the audit trail or make it clearer?
- Would a COHO or Arthur user switch to Yarro for this feature?
